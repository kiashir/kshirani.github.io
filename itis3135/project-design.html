Project: Fire Safety

Project overview:

  1. This website will be designed to help users learn about fire safety, different kind of combustion and safe ways of distinguishing them.
  2. The intended user of the website is any one who is interested about fire safety and wants to learn aboutn different kinds of fire.
  3. The website contains information about fire, causes of fire/combustion and safest ways of extinguishing fires.
